Roadmap

Execution Roadmap

How we systematically consult, select materials, fabricate, and install custom room settings.

Phase I

Discovery & Spatial Modeling

We begin with an in-depth spatial assessment. Our team analyzes room scale, traffic patterns, and light vectors to create architectural layout blueprints and 3D digital room models.

  • Scale & Proportion Auditing
  • Light Penetration Mapping
  • Clearance Clearance Blueprints
Phase II

Material & Palette Curation

We select the tactile components of your space. Accessing historical walnut wood mills, linen weaves, and custom metal casting catalogs, we construct unified material boards for color harmony.

  • Premium Wood Swatch Matching
  • Italian Wool & Linen Swatches
  • Custom Brass Hardware Selections
Phase III

Artisan Craft & Installation

Our Brooklyn woodworking atelier builds custom cabinetry, stitches premium fabrics, and assembles structures. We coordinate white-glove delivery, on-site assembly, and final styling.

  • Brooklyn Woodworking Atelier
  • Mortise & Tenon Joint Fabrication
  • White-Glove Staging & Delivery

Our Weekly Design Milestones

How we execute spatial styling tasks, deliver premium furniture pieces, and refine your room layout.

1. Curation Planning

Every Monday, we select custom styling tasks (e.g. wood matching, leather select) from our backlog and align on weekly priorities.

2. Continuous Crafting

During the week, we construct furniture frames, handcheck finishes, compile material palettes, and upload design renders.

3. Design & Quality Review

Every Friday, we perform meticulous structural quality inspections, evaluate finishes, and upload final photos directly to your design console.
